Good Roblox prototypes usually have a small loop that can be understood quickly: act, receive feedback, improve, repeat.

<TechBadgeGroup tags={["Luau", "Roblox Studio", "Gameplay"]} />

## Keep the Loop Tiny

For early prototypes, I try to keep the first loop small:

1. Give the player one clear action.
2. Show immediate feedback.
3. Reward progress.
4. Unlock one next choice.

<BlogCallout tone="lavender">
  If the first loop is fun with simple art, it usually survives visual polish.
</BlogCallout>
